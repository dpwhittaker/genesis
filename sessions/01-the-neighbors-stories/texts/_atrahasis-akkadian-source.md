## What is and isn't here (completeness)

eBL's Atraḫasīs edition is *in progress* (its own note: "An edition of this text is in
preparation by the eBL team. It will be uploaded in the course of 2025/2026."). As retrieved
on 2026-06-23, only some chapters carry text:

| Recension | Tablet | Status on eBL | Content |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Old Babylonian** | **I** | included below | gods' toil, the strike, the **creation of mankind**, start of overpopulation (321 verses, 6 columns) |
| Old Babylonian | II, III, Schøyen | empty placeholder | (the OB plagues & flood — not yet uploaded) |
| **Standard Babylonian** | **I, II, III** | included as SUPPLEMENT | later recension; II–III carry the **plagues and the flood** |
| Standard Babylonian | IV, V, VI, VIII, X | empty placeholder | |
| Middle Babylonian | Bab₂, Ug₂ | minor fragments, not included | |

Because the Old Babylonian **plague and flood** tablets (OB II, III) are not yet on eBL, the
**Standard Babylonian** Tablets I–III are appended **as a supplement** so the plague-and-flood
narrative is available. They are a **later recension** — clearly labelled, not Old Babylonian.

## Notation (eBL ATF)

Each verse appears as a **composite line** (`N. %n …`, normalized Akkadian, where `|` marks a
metrical foot and `||` a caesura) followed by one or more **manuscript transliteration lines**
(e.g. `SipOB1 i 12. is-qa₂-am id-du-u₂ …`, sign-by-sign, with column and line). Standard
sigla: `#` = damaged sign, `[ ]` = broken/restored, `x` = unread sign, `{d}` = divine
determinative, `*` = collation mark, `$ …` = structural/break note. eBL's own English
(`#tr.en:`) lines have been **removed** so this stays a pure Akkadian source for a fresh,
independent translation. `#note:` apparatus lines are kept.
